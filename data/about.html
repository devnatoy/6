About

Tomgu Food and Drinks Menu is a digital application designed to showcase the variety of food and beverage options offered by Tomgu. The app allows customers to conveniently browse through an organized menu that includes categories such as appetizers, main courses, desserts, and drinks. Each item on the menu includes a description, image, and pricing, helping users make informed choices. While this version of the app is for viewing only, it provides a clear and user-friendly interface for customers to explore the available offerings.
